Site Name

SZO Chamber Of Commerce. SZO means Sociedad de Zona Oeste which represents the part of Buenos Aires commerces are located.

Site Purpose

SZO Chamber of Commerce aims to improve member productivity through communication, teaching, and collaboration for both long-standing and new members. The purpose of this website is to add more businesses and companies to a network that connects and shares the benefits that each member can offer as well as receive.

Scenarios

How can joining SZO help me improve the productivity of my business?

Thanks to the different members who are part of the chamber, you will be able to learn different tools that will help you improve your sales and expand your client portfolio. We will be providing courses that focus specifically on what has worked for today's businesses and companies that belong to the Western area of Buenos Aires.

What do long-time members gain by sharing their success tips in SZO?

Although it may be difficult to understand, the former members have decided to share what has helped them achieve success in the West area because they have a deep love for this part of Buenos Aires and, if there is great growth in business in general, they also benefit due to the increase in customers and becoming a busier area.

Is there a maximum member capacity?

No. Any business or company that is interested in helping and at the same time improving their venture can easily join SZO.

Color Schema
